How is Your Icons different from other packs?

Well, this set has a number of advantages over others

1) There are a lot of them. Really a lot. 7000+ icons, this is enough for any, even the most specific cases.

2) Six styles. There is no project in which these icons would not be useful.

3) Super ellipses. All the icons are not just rounded, they have a special shape called "Superelipse", which attracts any eye and makes you wonder: "Why does she look so unusual?"

4) Ready for handover for development. All icons are vector, you don't have to do anything for developers to use them comfortably in development

5) Well, they are just beautiful :D

In fact, it would take a very long time to list how many advantages Solar has, but we suggest that you simply use them and enjoy them.

Where can I use Solar?

You can use icons anywhere: in print, in books, in interfaces, as a print on clothes or mugs, in general, everywhere, you are only limited by your imagination.

Also, the 480 Design team would really appreciate it if you could mention us in Your projects <3

How can I custumize Your icons?

Everythng could be done right inside of Figma. You can easily change icon stroke, colours and even add effects in the bottom of right sidebar.

Moreover, You can easily combine styles like stroke + line duotone.

For perfect tuning, select the shape and hit Enter on the keyboard - after that You can do whatever want: change form of icon, way of vector points.
